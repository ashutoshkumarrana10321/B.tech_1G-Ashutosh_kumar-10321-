Rungta InternationalSkillsUniversity

Rungta College is a group of educational institutions in Chhattisgarh, India, known for offering a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, pharmacy, and more.

It includes institutions like Rungta College of Engineering and Technology (RCET) and Rungta International University (RIU), which focuses on skill development and has NAAC A Grade accreditation. The campuses feature extensive facilities, with a strong emphasis on research, industry collaborations, and student activities.
